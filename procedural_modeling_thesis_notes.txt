MASTER’S THESIS PREPARATION NOTES Interactive Urban Space Design Using
Procedural Modeling

  --------------------------------------------------
  1. CORE CONCEPT
  --------------------------------------------------
  Procedural modeling combines: - Rule-based systems
  (grammars, rules) - Parameters (controllable
  values)

  Rules define structure (e.g., building → floors →
  windows) Parameters control variation (e.g.,
  height, number of floors)

  Your thesis extends this with: - Differentiable
  modeling - Inverse design (geometry → parameters)
